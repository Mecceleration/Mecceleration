ABOUT US

Mindsparks is the signature event of AUST Innovation and Design Club. Mindspark began its journey with a single goal of unifying the young minds of the country from diverse engineering and business departments under one roof. It is a national event that is organized in association with IIT, Roorkee, India. This is the biggest competition organized in AUST comprising segments from both the Engineering Departments and also the School of Business. After the international collaboration made between AUSTIDC and IIT, Roorkee of India, the winners of some segments get the chance to participate in Cognizance, a signature event of IIT with all expenses born by the organizers. Two versions of this event, in 2019 and 2020 have already been organized with the hope for more in the future.
