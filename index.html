MECCELERATION

The biggest mechanical engineering Tech-Biz competition in Bangladesh is back with a bang after 2 years! Whether you are an aspiring engineer looking to stack your machines/ robots up against the very best, an aspiring entrepreneur, the local brainiac or the best gamer your friends know- Mecceleration 2022 has a proving ground ready for you! Fight for glory in one of our 11 signature events and aspire to be the best of a thousand participants. Red Heaven awaits you. Registrations are open now!

DEPARTMENT OF MECHANICAL AND PRODUCTION ENGINEERING

For the 7th time in almost as many years, the Department of Mechanical and Production Engineering (MPE), IUT is set to host Mecceleration, the biggest Mechanical Engineering technology; innovation; and business festival in Bangladesh. The Department of Mechanical and Production Engineering (MPE) is an academic department of the Islamic University of Technology (IUT) established to help develop human resources in the member states of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation(OIC). The Department is also involved in technological and engineering research as well as in organizing seminars, short courses.
